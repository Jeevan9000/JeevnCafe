Our Story

From a garage roaster to the city's favourite cup.

The Beginning

Started by two friends
and one broken espresso machine.

In 2019, Riya Kapoor and Dev Anand set up a second-hand La Marzocco in their Indiranagar garage and started roasting Ethiopian beans on a popcorn machine. Neighbours complained about the smell — but then started showing up with cups.

That garage became a stall, the stall became a café, and today Brewnara occupies a beautiful 1940s heritage building on Lavelle Road, serving over 300 cups a day.

We still source directly from the same farms in Yirgacheffe. We still roast every Tuesday and Friday. And we still believe that coffee is best when you know the whole story behind it.

Dev pulling shots, 2022

FAQ

Got questions?

Do you offer dairy-free milk alternatives?

Yes! We carry oat milk, almond milk, and soy milk. All plant milks are an additional ₹40 per drink.

Can I buy your beans online?

Absolutely. We ship nationally and offer monthly subscriptions. Visit our bean shop page or DM us on Instagram.

Do you take table reservations?

For groups of 4 or more, yes! Call us or email hello@brewnara.in. Walk-ins always welcome for smaller groups.

Is there WiFi and good seating for work?

Yes — free WiFi, power sockets at every table, and very good coffee to keep you going. We ask that laptops wrap up by 5 PM on weekends to free space.

What's the reusable cup discount?

Bring any clean, reusable cup and we'll knock 10% off your drink. It's our small contribution to less waste.

Do you cater for corporate events?

Yes — espresso bar pop-ups, filter coffee catering, and bean gift sets for offices. Email events@brewnara.in.
